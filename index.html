Gold Rush!!

You will be given a target weight for your gold collection at the start of the game

There are four gold nuggets below. By clicking ona nugget you will add a specific amount of weight to your collection.

You win the game by matching your total collection to the target weight. If you exceed your target gold weight, you'll lose.

The weight of each nugget is hidden until you click it.

Each new game will have new values.
